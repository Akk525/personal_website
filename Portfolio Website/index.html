About

Aspiring Innovator in AI: Harnessing the Power of Technology to Shape the Future

My name is Aditya Kuniyil Kattil, and I am a sophomore studying Computer Science at Purdue University. Originally from Bengaluru, I have always been passionate about exploring the field of computer science, particularly in developing user-friendly software to address everyday problems. This vision has guided my journey over the past six years, during which I have learned and created applications with modern user interfaces.

I began with web development and then expanded my skills to programming in C, Java, and Python. Recently, my interests have grown to include blockchain development and Artificial Intelligence, leading me to learn Solidity and PyTorch. Additionally, I have experience in video editing, creating computer graphics, and basic 3D modeling.

I believe I am a hardworking individual with an ever-growing curiosity to learn more. Feel free to connect with me to discuss potential collaborations or just to share ideas!
